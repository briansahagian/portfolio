Project Overview

Complete precision machining project involving the design and manufacture of a chess piece using CNC turning and milling operations. This comprehensive project demonstrated advanced machining skills through careful programming, setup, and execution of complex geometries requiring tight tolerances and excellent surface finishes. The work included CAD design, G-code programming, workholding strategies, tool selection, and quality control procedures. The project showcased understanding of manufacturing processes, precision measurement, and attention to detail required for high-quality machined components.
