1969 Honda CB350 Complete Rebuild

Full engine teardown, restoration, and rebuild of a classic motorcycle with comprehensive documentation

Personal Project Individual Work Summer Project

Project Overview

Complete engine rebuild of a 1969 Honda CB350 motorcycle from non-running, seized condition to fully operational state. This comprehensive restoration project involved complete engine disassembly, professional machine work, precision rebuilding, and successful restoration to running condition. The project required extensive mechanical knowledge, patience, and attention to detail while working with vintage motorcycle components and limited documentation. Over the course of a full summer, I successfully freed the seized engine, restored all critical components, and rebuilt the entire powerplant to factory specifications.
