Project Overview

A key responsibility of every EPIC employee is maintaining operational equipment through repair, preventative maintenance, and regular cleaning procedures. Through the Adopt-A-Tool program, each advisor is assigned a specific machine to monitor and maintain. I was personally assigned the horizontal bandsaw, taking full responsibility for its upkeep and ensuring consistent operational readiness. This gallery documents my bandsaw maintenance work alongside cold saw repair projects I completed, showcasing the comprehensive machine care responsibilities essential to maintaining a productive and efficient machine shop environment.
