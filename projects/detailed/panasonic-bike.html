Vintage Panasonic Bike Restoration

Complete mechanical restoration and customization of vintage Panasonic bicycle with modern components and classic styling

Personal Project Individual Work Multi-week Build

Project Overview

Complete mechanical restoration and customization of a vintage Panasonic bicycle, transforming it into a reliable and stylish modern bike while preserving its classic aesthetic. This comprehensive project involved frame restoration, drivetrain upgrades, component selection and installation, and mechanical system optimization. The work required understanding of bicycle mechanics, component compatibility, restoration techniques, and integration of modern components with vintage frames. Through careful planning and execution, the project successfully created a functional bicycle that combines vintage styling with modern mechanical reliability and performance.
