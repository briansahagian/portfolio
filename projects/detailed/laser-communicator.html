Laser Communication System

Wireless communication system using modulated laser light for audio transmission across open air gaps

Class Project Team Project Semester Project

Project Overview

Designed and constructed a wireless communication system that transmits audio signals using modulated laser light. This innovative project demonstrated the principles of optical communication by successfully transmitting clear audio across open air gaps without physical connections. The system incorporated laser modulation, photodetector circuits, amplification systems, and signal processing to achieve reliable voice and music transmission. Through careful engineering of both transmitter and receiver circuits, the team created a functional demonstration of optical communication technology used in modern fiber optic and free-space optical communication systems.
