HDPE Coaster CNC Milling

Complete CAD-to-CAM workflow using HAAS CNC mills and HSMWorks for precision coaster manufacturing

Machine Shop Project Individual Work Multi-day Project

Project Overview

This comprehensive CNC machining project demonstrates the complete CAD-to-CAM-to-CNC workflow using professional HAAS machining centers and advanced HSMWorks CAM software. The project focuses on precision manufacturing of HDPE coasters with complex geometries, showcasing proficiency in toolpath optimization, cutting parameter selection, and advanced manufacturing techniques.

Working with High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE), this project required careful consideration of material properties, thermal management during machining, and specialized tooling strategies to achieve excellent surface finish and dimensional accuracy. The manufacturing process demonstrates both technical machining skills and understanding of polymer machining best practices.

Project Specifications

Material: HDPE Plastic
Machine: HAAS VF-2 VMC
CAM Software: HSMWorks/Fusion CAM
Tolerances: ±0.01"
Surface Finish: 125 μin Ra

Technical Implementation Details

Material Considerations

HDPE machining required specialized techniques and considerations:

  • Low melting point requiring optimized cutting speeds
  • Tendency to weld to cutting tools - specialized tool coatings
  • Chip evacuation strategies for stringy material behavior
  • Thermal management to prevent dimensional distortion

CAM Programming Excellence

HSMWorks programming focused on efficiency and quality:

  • Adaptive clearing for optimal material removal rates
  • Scallop finishing for superior surface quality
  • Optimized tool engagement for tool life
  • Collision detection and avoidance strategies
