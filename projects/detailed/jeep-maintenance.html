Jeep Wrangler Maintenance

Comprehensive automotive maintenance and repair on 2001 Jeep Wrangler including brake system service, gasket replacement, and routine maintenance procedures

Personal Project Individual Work Ongoing Maintenance

Project Overview

This ongoing automotive maintenance project involves comprehensive care and repair of a 2001 Jeep Wrangler, focusing on both preventive maintenance and corrective repairs. The project has included brake system service, gasket replacements, fluid changes, and various mechanical repairs to maintain vehicle reliability and performance.

Through hands-on automotive repair work, I've developed skills in automotive troubleshooting, brake system maintenance, engine service, and general mechanical repair while maintaining detailed maintenance records and following proper safety procedures.

Vehicle Specifications

  • Vehicle: 2001 Jeep Wrangler TJ
  • Engine: 4.0L inline-6 cylinder
  • Transmission: Manual 5-speed
  • Drive System: 4WD with manual transfer case
  • Maintenance Type: Preventive and corrective repairs

Maintenance Procedures

  1. Diagnostic Assessment: Comprehensive vehicle inspection and issue identification
  2. Brake System Service: Brake pad replacement, rotor resurfacing, and fluid service
  3. Engine Maintenance: Oil changes, filter replacements, and gasket repairs
  4. Cooling System: Coolant service and radiator maintenance
  5. Preventive Care: Routine maintenance scheduling and component inspection
  6. Documentation: Maintenance record keeping and cost tracking

Key Achievements

  • Successfully completed brake system overhaul with improved stopping performance
  • Performed engine gasket replacements eliminating oil leaks
  • Implemented comprehensive preventive maintenance schedule
  • Improved vehicle reliability and extended component lifespan
  • Developed automotive troubleshooting and repair skills
  • Maintained detailed service records and cost analysis
