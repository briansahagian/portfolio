Complete design and analysis of gasket manufacturing process including material selection, tooling design, and quality control procedures

Project Overview

Comprehensive design and analysis of a complete gasket manufacturing process from material selection through final quality control. This project involved detailed engineering analysis of manufacturing methods, tooling design, process optimization, and quality assurance procedures for automotive gasket production. The work demonstrated understanding of manufacturing engineering principles, material science applications, and industrial process design through systematic analysis of each production step, cost optimization strategies, and implementation of statistical quality control methods.
